16 Jan 2014

| 6 min read
As few of You know I have solved a lot of Unblock Me puzzles and posted cheats at Unblock Me Solutions.

Pathuku - Connecting The Lines!

It is much interesting for me and started solving a few of them. It is extremely simple and extremely tricky too! You can add this puzzle to Your chrome browser and you can play yourself for a few minutes.
