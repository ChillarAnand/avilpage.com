21 Jan 2014

About Little Alchemy:

You start with four basic elements, and mix and match them to create more and more awesome things.Combine the elements by simply dragging and dropping one on the other to make something new. See how many elements you can come up with. There are 480 elements to create. How many can you make?You can synchronize your progress with the Chrome and web version. Just click 'Login' and use your Google account to save and load the library. You can also use it to have your library on different computers!
