Watch YouTube Videos Without Buffering On A Slow Internet Connection

So, You have a slow internet connection and You want to watch your favorite videos without buffering? Here is the trick. One thing you can do immediately increase buffering speed is to reduce the quality of the video.

The other thing you can do is to reduce the information on the page other than video. To do this, you can go to the bottom of the page and click on " Try something new "  In the next page you will find " Youtube feather " If you opt in for the Youtube feather, you can watch videos without buffering even with a slow internet connection. However the comments under the video and some other features will be missing!

Watch the video to know how to do that!

Using Webcam In Ubuntu - Cheese Installation

I want to learn about Ubuntu. So, I started geeking with that. Form now, if there are any useful tips, I will share with you. There are a lot of apps, which need webcam. So, I want to use webcam. Here is what I have done. Here I am showing how to use inbuilt webcam in Ubuntu 12.04.

Open terminal ( Shortcut: Ctrl + Alt + T ).
Type: " sudo apt-get install cheese " and hit enter.

Cheese will be installed. It will take around 3 minutes ( around 18MB )depending on your net speed. Once it is completed. Search for " cheese. " You can start recording any videos. Hope that helps.
