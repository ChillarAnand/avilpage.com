Watch YouTube Videos Without Buffering On A Slow Internet Connection

So, You have a slow internet connection and You want to watch your favorite videos without buffering? Here is the trick. One thing you can do immediately increase buffering speed is to reduce the quality of the video.

The other thing you can do is to reduce the information on the page other than video. To do this, you can go to the bottom of the page and click on " Try something new "  In the next page you will find " Youtube feather " If you opt in for the Youtube feather, you can watch videos without buffering even with a slow internet connection.
