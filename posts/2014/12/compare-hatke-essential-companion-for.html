There are a lot of price comparison Chrome extensions like MakkiChoose, Pricekart, Ciuvo price comparision & ValueTag for Indian online stores. But Compare Hatke provides some additional features to make online shopping a bit easier.

Just like other extensions it also compares price of products from various stores. You can even set a price drop alert, which notifies You when the price of product is dropped. In addition to that it also searches for available coupons and applies them when You checkout. It also has a price graph.

If you go to any product page on any website & if You scroll down there will be a price graph which shows the price variation of the product. Based on that You can decide whether You can buy it immediately or wait for a few days. Below that graph it shows how favorable it is to buy that product today, within a week or within a month.

If You do a lot of online shopping, this plugin comes in handy to get a product at a cheaper rate and decide when to buy the product.
